## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am an MPhil researcher in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Salford, focused on building practical and trustworthy AI systems.

My current research explores multimodal recommender systems, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), grounded explanations, and agentic AI workflows.

I enjoy building real-world AI pipelines that combine research ideas with production-ready engineering. My projects usually involve recommender systems, LLM applications, FastAPI backends, multimodal models, and practical AI deployment.
